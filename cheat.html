4 PICS 1 WORD

A Cheat Sheet



A. All words included in this game range from 3-6 letters.

B. For items no. 1-5, the answers revolve around Computer Science.

C. For items no. 6-10, the answers pertain to Biology.

D. Definitions of each item:

FOR COMPUTER SCIENCE-RELATED ITEMS:

1. Refers to information processed or stored by a computer and may be in the form of text documents, images, audio clips, software programs, or other types.

ANSWER: DATA

2. Refers to an interpreted, object-oriented, high-level programming language with dynamic semantics. This programming language was developed by Guido van Rossum.

ANSWER: PYTHON

3. It is referred to as a failure or a flaw in the software program which produces an incorrect or undesired result that deviates from the expected result or behavior.

ANSWER: BUG

4. Refers to a list of commands that are executed by a certain program or scripting engine. This may be used to automate processes on a local computer or to generate Web pages on the Web.

ANSWER: SCRIPT

5. Refers to the component within your computer that allows for short-term data access. Data stored within this is accessed by the computer in order to perform many operations.

ANSWER: MEMORY


FOR BIOLOGY-RELATED ITEMS:

6. Pertains to any member of the group of vertebrate animals in which the young are nourished with milk from special mammary glands of the mother. Examples are elephants, giraffes, lions, and tigers.

ANSWER: MAMMAL

7. Refers to any group of spore-producing organisms feeding on organic matter. These include molds, yeast, mushrooms, and toadstools.

ANSWER: FUNGUS

8. Pertains to an infectious agent of small size and simple composition that can multiply only in living cells of animals, plants, or bacteria but cannot reproduce by itself. The name is from a Latin word meaning “slimy liquid” or “poison.”

ANSWER: VIRUS

9. Refers to a large community of vegetation and wildlife adapted to a specific climate. The five major types include are aquatic, grassland, forest, desert, and tundra.

ANSWER: BIOME

10. It is the study of organisms in the kingdom Plantae, otherwise known as plants.

ANSWER: BOTANY
